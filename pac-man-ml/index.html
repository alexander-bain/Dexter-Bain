How It Learns

  1. Play manually with arrows or WASD.
  2. Every turn creates a training sample automatically.
  3. Dots, survival, energizers, and bonuses reward recent moves.
  4. Getting caught penalizes the recent moves that led there.
  5. If the ML bot loses all lives, it starts a new run by itself.
  6. Press M or the ML Bot Turn button to let it play.
