Omnis: Shattered Convergence

A compressed arcade slice of the impossible mega-game: neon city, voxel mining, monster capsules, arena storm, shooter spells, and a nexus war all fighting for the same screen.

  • Move with WASD or the touch pad. Aim by moving. Space uses the active tool.
  • Rifle hurts drones, staff blasts raid bosses, build places concrete walls.
  • Touch wild elementals to capture them. Destroy the nexus before the storm closes.
